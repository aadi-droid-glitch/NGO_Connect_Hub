# NGO_Connect_Hub
A platform which acts a bridge between the Users who are looking for NGO's to volunteer or donate their help and Ngo's who want to list themself and seeks help of others as Volunteer. 
It helps the Volunteer to locate a NGO near them and participate for upcoming events and as for NGO , it keeps a track of all their members, but not just that , it also posts upcoming events with all the details through which Volunteer can join and participate.
